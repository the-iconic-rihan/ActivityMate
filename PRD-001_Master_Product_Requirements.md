# 1. Product Vision

Build India's most trusted platform for discovering real-world activity
partners.

This is **not** a dating platform.

The platform exists to help people participate in experiences they would
otherwise skip because they have nobody to go with.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Every day thousands of people move to cities like Mumbai.

Common scenarios:

- New employee
- Student
- Solo traveller
- Freelancer
- Remote worker
- Recently relocated family

These users often have free time but no trusted people to join them.

Current alternatives include: - Waiting for office colleagues - WhatsApp
groups - Facebook groups - Instagram posts - Reddit communities - Travel
agencies - Meetup apps - Dating apps

None are optimized for spontaneous, trusted activity matching.

---

# 3. Goals

## Business Goals

- Launch in Mumbai
- Reach product-market fit
- Build trust-first community
- Expand city by city

## User Goals

- Find trusted companions
- Avoid going alone for night activities post 10pm in mumbai and other cities
- Build local social circles
- Participate safely

---
