## Purpose

Ongoing qualitative research infrastructure — a standing practice, not a one-time report.

## Why this folder exists

Market research answers 'is there a market'; user research answers 'what exactly do we build for them' and keeps running after launch. Keeping them separate stops one-off market validation from being mistaken for continuous product research.
