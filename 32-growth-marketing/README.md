## Purpose

Acquisition strategy and launch playbooks. Corresponds to PRD-016 and PRD-020.

## Why this folder exists

Growth strategy for a trust-first product cannot simply copy paid-acquisition dating-app playbooks — trust products grow through host-led organic loops, where a good host brings their own circle — so growth mechanics need their own reasoning here, not an assumption borrowed from an adjacent category.
