# Compensation Equity Framework

**Status:** Draft v1 — illustrative bands, to be replaced with real market-benchmarked figures before use in an actual offer.

## Scope

Pay bands and the equity-grant framework.

## Principles

1. **Bands are set by role and level, not negotiated ad hoc.** Ad hoc negotiation systematically disadvantages candidates who negotiate less aggressively, which correlates with factors we don't want compensation to be a function of. Every offer is drawn from a band; exceptions require founder sign-off and a documented reason.
2. **Cash comp is benchmarked to Mumbai/Bangalore tech market rates for early-stage startups**, not discounted purely because we're pre-Series-A — we compete for the same engineers as funded Series A/B companies, and equity alone does not close that gap for most candidates.
3. **Equity is a genuine ownership stake tied to level and joining stage**, front-loaded for earlier hires who take on more risk, and standardized with a 4-year vest and 1-year cliff (industry-standard ESOP structure under Indian company law).
4. **Trust & Safety roles are banded on par with equivalent-level engineering/product roles**, not treated as a lower-cost operations function — reflecting the org's stated view (see [culture-values-handbook.md](culture-values-handbook.md)) that trust & safety is core infrastructure, not a cost center.

## Illustrative cash bands (Mumbai, pre-Series-A, annual CTC in ₹ lakhs)

| Level | Backend/Mobile Engineer | Trust & Safety Analyst | Growth/Community | Design |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| L1 (0–2 yrs) | 8–12 | 7–10 | 6–9 | 7–10 |
| L2 (2–5 yrs) | 14–20 | 11–15 | 10–14 | 12–16 |
| L3 (5–8 yrs, senior/lead) | 22–32 | 16–22 | 15–20 | 18–25 |
| L4 (8+ yrs, head of function) | 35–50+ | 24–35 | 22–32 | 28–38 |

Founding-team and Phase-0 hires (see [hiring-plan-org-chart.md](hiring-plan-org-chart.md)) are typically negotiated individually below these bands in exchange for higher equity, which is standard for a pre-seed team and should be documented separately, not folded into these bands.

## Illustrative equity grant guidelines (% of fully diluted cap table, at grant)

| Level / stage joined | Illustrative equity range |
|---|---|
| Founding team (Phase 0) | Per [cap-table.md](../35-investor-relations/cap-table.md) founder allocation |
| Early hire, Phase 0–1 (first ~10 hires) | 0.25% – 1.5%, scaled by level and role criticality |
| Phase 2 hire (post public launch) | 0.05% – 0.5% |
| Phase 3+ hire (Series A readiness onward) | 0.01% – 0.15% |

Standard terms: 4-year vest, 1-year cliff, standard exercise window (recommend extending beyond the typical 90-day post-termination window to reduce the "pay to keep options" pressure on departing employees who leave in good standing — a specific, deliberate choice to be confirmed with counsel).

## Review cadence

- Cash bands reviewed annually against market data, and whenever a role proves hard to fill at the current band (a signal the band is stale, not that the candidate pool is weak).
- Equity ranges reviewed at every funding round, since dilution and 409A-equivalent valuation (fair market value for Indian ESOP purposes) change the real value of a given percentage.
- Any exception to a band (above or below) requires written justification and founder approval, logged for future band recalibration.
