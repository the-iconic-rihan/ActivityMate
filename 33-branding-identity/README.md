## Purpose

Brand positioning and voice, kept separate from the visual design system.

## Why this folder exists

Split from design-system because brand strategy (words, positioning) and the visual system (tokens, components) have different owners and different review cadences — marketing iterates on messaging far more often than the component library changes.
